North London

Finsbury Park Tennis Courts

A friendly, welcoming, non-pretentious tennis venue in North London. We’re looking for like minded folk who love tennis. We run an all year coaching programme for those who want to socialize, play competitively, get some exercise or just dip their toe in the water. We welcome complete beginners to seasoned pros and everything in between. There are no prohibitive members fees to pay to book a court. We now have flood lights for 6 of our courts. There is an additional £4 charge for bookings that require the use of the floodlights. Courts can be booked up to 7 days in advance, courts are released at midnight for the 7th day. Website: www.finsburyparktennis.org.uk Email: info@finsburyparktennis.org.uk Address: Finsbury Park, N4 3ET

East London

West Ham Park Tennis

West Ham Park, the largest park in the borough of Newham, has 12 tennis courts. The facilities are carefully maintained by a dedicated team of City of London park keepers making West Ham Park one of the most popular tennis facilities in East London. The annual tennis membership allows you and your family members to play tennis on the courts for free throughout the year. In addition to the free courts booking, you will enjoy the tennis coaching offers and discounts for active members. The Annual Pass is simple and a great value for money for individuals and families, making tennis accessible and affordable for all the community. Website: www.clubspark.lta.org.uk/westhampark Email: westhampark@cityoflondon.gov.uk Address: Upton Lane, E7 9PU

South London

All Star Tennis, Wandsworth Common

Everyone is welcome to play on the All Star Tennis courts across Wandsworth and in doing so joins the All Star community as member – free of charge. There are 6 all-weather courts and a full junior and adult programme in Wandsworth Common. Overall, we have 28 courts across 6 locations in Wandsworth. Put your game to the test in competitions throughout the year in our Adult tournaments or focus on the areas of your game you wish to improve with our tennis coaches. People looking to play more regularly can join the Member Plus scheme and enjoy discounts on all bookings as well as 14 day advanced court booking. Website: www.allstartennis.co.uk Email: info@allstartennis.co.uk Address: Dorlcote road, SW18 3RT

West London

Queen’s Park Tennis Courts

Queen’s Park is a fine example of a well-loved and hugely popular city park. It provides a welcome splash of green in one of the most densely populated parts of London, serving the residents of the London Borough of Brent in which it is located, as well as Londoners in general. Facilities include six all-weather tennis courts. Annual membership for £31.60 will allow you to book courts up to 7 days in advance and will allow you to play more tennis more often. Courts can be booked online using ClubSpark. First you need to register, then select for one of our packages and then visit the booking page to play. Website: www.clubspark.lta.org.uk/queensparktenniscourts Email: queens.park@cityoflondon.gov.uk Address: Queens Park, NW6 6SG
